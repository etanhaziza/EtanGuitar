How Lessons Work

Learning to play the guitar should be an experience that your child finds stimulating, challenging, and...FUN!

Learn Music You Are Interested In

Learning to play the guitar is much more fun when you actually like what you're playing.

Lessons are focused on teaching music fundamentals (theory, technique, etc.) through using the music that your child likes to listen to.

Regular Parent Updates

Frequent updates about what your child is working on, how they are progressing, and how you can help them practice more efficiently.

Follow Up Video After the Lesson

Learning something new is challenging! It's always helpful to have a short video example explaining exactly what was covered in the lesson.
